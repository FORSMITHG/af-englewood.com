Angstrom Fiber Englewood is growing. We're looking for manufacturing teammates, technicians, and engineers who want to be part of a Tier 1 automotive supplier with a global network behind them.

Why Angstrom

A local facility with
global opportunity.

Working at Angstrom Fiber Englewood means being part of Angstrom Automotive Group — a $500M+ global manufacturer with 15 companies, 33+ locations, and 4,000+ employees worldwide. You get the stability and resources of an enterprise, with the community of a locally-operated Englewood facility.

We operate in a purpose-built, modern manufacturing environment — 105,000+ sq. ft. built in 2017 — with state-of-the-art compression molding, vacuum forming, and robotic waterjet equipment. We invest in our people and our plant.

Role Types

Where you'd fit in.

Production Teammates

Processing and assembling parts through compression molding and vacuum forming. No experience required — we train. First and second shift available.

Maintenance & Technicians

Skilled trades, maintenance technicians, and PLC/controls experience valued. Hydraulic and pneumatic systems, Allen Bradley experience a plus.

Engineering

Manufacturing, quality, and process engineers supporting PPAP, DFMEA, and continuous improvement. Plastics or composites background preferred.

Operations & Supervision

Production supervisors and team leaders overseeing molding and assembly operations. Automotive manufacturing experience valued.

Finance & Administration

Plant controller, accounting, and administrative support roles. Financial reporting, budgeting, and payroll experience for manufacturing environments.

Quality

Quality engineers, inspectors, and PPAP coordinators. IATF 16949 awareness required. Core tools (PFMEA, Control Plan, MSA) experience valued.

Career Growth
