Who We Are

Angstrom Fiber Group at Englewood, Ohio.

Angstrom Fiber Englewood is a division of Angstrom Fiber Group under Angstrom Automotive Group — a NMSDC-certified minority-owned Tier 1 supplier with 15 companies across North America, South America, and Europe.

Our 105,000+ sq. ft. Englewood facility has manufactured acoustics, airflow, water management, and structural fiber components for major OEMs since 2018. We joined the Angstrom Fiber Group family in 2023, bringing global engineering resources and expanded customer reach to our Dayton-area plant.

Core Capabilities

Built for every corner of the vehicle.

Three core manufacturing processes — compression molding, vacuum forming, and fiber processing — backed by robotic waterjet, assembly, and full PPAP capability.

Compression Molding

Heat and pressure forming of fiber-reinforced composites, SMC, and GMT materials to net-shape automotive parts.

Vacuum Forming

Thermoforming and vacuum forming of plastic and composite sheet materials into complex automotive geometries.

Fiber Processing

Polyester and recycled fiber production, layup, and forming for NVH, underbody, and interior applications.

Robotic Waterjet Cutting

5-axis robotic waterjet trimming for precision edge profiles and complex three-dimensional geometries.

Assembly & Sub-Assembly

Lamination, hardware attachment, kitting, and OEM-compliant packaging for just-in-time delivery.

Engineering & PPAP

On-site engineering for DFMEA, prototype development, PPAP documentation, and customer-specific validation.

Sustainability

Recycled materials.
Production-grade results.

Our manufacturing philosophy is built on a green-first foundation — producing high-performance automotive components from recycled and reclaimed materials that would otherwise enter the waste stream. That commitment shapes every material decision we make.

Angstrom Group extends this across all locations through LED lighting conversion, supply chain footprint optimization, and active investment in EV-compatible NVH and thermal management components.
