About

Part of the Angstrom Automotive Group network.

Angstrom Fiber Englewood has operated at 300 Lau Pkwy since 2018 as a division of Angstrom Fiber Group under Angstrom Automotive Group — a NMSDC-certified minority-owned manufacturer with facilities across North America and Europe. Our Englewood facility brings enterprise-scale manufacturing capability to a focused, locally-operated plant serving the Midwest automotive corridor.

Formerly operating as Hematite Englewood and Woodbridge Englewood, this location carries a long-standing manufacturing heritage rooted in a commitment to using recycled and reclaimed materials to produce high-quality automotive components.

Capabilities

What we do at this facility.

Raw Material Production

Polyester fiber production from recycled and virgin feedstocks, processed to automotive-grade specifications.

Fiber Forming

Controlled fiber layup and forming that produces consistent density, weight, and acoustic performance.

Compression Molding

Heat and pressure molding to net-shape, producing dimensionally stable parts ready for vehicle installation.

Robotic Waterjet Cutting

Precision robotic waterjet trimming for complex geometries and tight-tolerance edge profiles.

Assembly

Sub-assembly and final assembly including lamination, attachment hardware, and OEM-compliant packaging.

Engineering Support

On-site engineering resources for DFMEA, PPAP, and prototype development alongside your design team.

Products

Fiber components across the vehicle.

Our Englewood facility produces fiber-based components used throughout the passenger vehicle interior and underbody — engineered for weight, acoustics, and durability.

  • Wheel Liners Molded fiber wheel arch liners for underbody protection and NVH reduction
  • Interior Headliners Lightweight fiber substrate headliner assemblies with class-A finish compatibility
  • Trunk Liners Formed fiber cargo and trunk trim panels
  • Underbody Protection Fiber-based underbody shields for thermal and acoustic management
  • NVH Materials Noise, vibration, and harshness absorption components for interior applications

Sustainability

Recycled materials.
Production-grade results.

Our manufacturing approach is built on a green-first philosophy — producing high-performance fiber components using materials that would otherwise enter the waste stream, without sacrificing dimensional accuracy, acoustic performance, or OEM quality standards.

Angstrom Group extends this commitment across all locations through LED conversion programs, supply chain footprint optimization, and investment in EV-compatible component development.
