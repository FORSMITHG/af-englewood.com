Products

Acoustic. Thermal.
Structural. Molded.

Our Englewood facility produces fiber and molded components used throughout the vehicle — interior, underbody, and under-hood — for acoustics, airflow, water management, and structure.

Product Range

Components we manufacture.

All products are manufactured using recycled and virgin fiber feedstocks, thermoplastic sheet, SMC, or GMT composite materials through compression molding, vacuum forming, or fiber processing. Custom programs welcome.

Wheel Liners

Compression molded or vacuum formed wheel arch liners for underbody protection, water deflection, and NVH reduction. Available in fiber, thermoplastic, or composite construction.

Interior Headliners

Trunk & Cargo Liners

Molded cargo area trim and trunk liner panels with durable surface finish and acoustic backing. Custom geometry per program specification.

Underbody Shields

Materials

A full tool box
of materials.

Angstrom Fiber Englewood works with thermoplastic sheet, sheet molding compound (SMC), glass mat thermoplastic (GMT), polyester fiber, recycled fiber blends, PVC-based composites, and non-woven fabric substrates — giving customers engineering flexibility without facility changes.

All product programs are available with recycled content options consistent with our green-first manufacturing mission. EV-compatible thermal and acoustic material variants available.
