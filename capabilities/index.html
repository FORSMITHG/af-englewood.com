Manufacturing Processes

What we do, and how we do it.

Compression Molding

High-pressure forming of fiber-reinforced composites, sheet molding compound (SMC), and glass mat thermoplastic (GMT) to net-shape automotive parts. Capable of complex geometry, tight tolerances, and high-volume repeatability.

Vacuum Forming

Thermoforming and vacuum forming of thermoplastic sheet and composite materials into three-dimensional automotive geometries. Ideal for large-area interior and underbody panels with complex surface detail.

Fiber Processing

Polyester and recycled-material fiber production, controlled layup, and forming for NVH, underbody protection, acoustic absorption, and interior lining applications. Our green manufacturing foundation since 1978.

Robotic Waterjet Cutting

Precision 5-axis robotic waterjet trimming for complex geometries, tight-tolerance edge profiles, and repeatable cut quality on molded and formed parts. No tooling wear, no heat distortion.

Assembly & Sub-Assembly

Lamination, adhesive bonding, hardware attachment, kitting, and OEM-compliant packaging. We deliver assemblies ready for vehicle installation, reducing your line-side complexity.

Engineering & Quality

On-site engineering for DFMEA, prototype development, and PPAP documentation. Full quality management aligned with IATF 16949. Customer-specific requirements met or exceeded.
