About Us

Angstrom Fiber Englewood.
Tier 1. Dayton, Ohio.

We are a division of Angstrom Fiber Group under Angstrom Automotive Group — a globally operating Tier 1 supplier manufacturing compression molded, vacuum formed, and fiber-based NVH components from our facility at 300 Lau Pkwy, Englewood, Ohio.

Our Story

Part of Angstrom Group.
Operating in Englewood.

Angstrom Fiber Englewood is a division of Angstrom Fiber Group under Angstrom Automotive Group — a NMSDC-certified minority-owned Tier 1 supplier with 15 companies and 33+ locations across North America, South America, and Europe. We joined the Angstrom family in 2023.

Our 105,000+ sq. ft. Englewood facility has been manufacturing NVH, acoustic, airflow, and structural fiber components for major OEMs since opening in 2018. The facility became Angstrom Fiber Englewood in 2023, bringing expanded engineering resources and a global customer network to our locally-operated Dayton-area plant.

The green-first manufacturing philosophy embedded in this facility — producing automotive-grade components from recycled and reclaimed materials — is a core part of the Angstrom Group's sustainability commitment across all locations.

Facility History

How we got here.

The Englewood facility has operated under three names since opening in 2018. The capabilities, equipment, and team are continuous — the name that matters today is Angstrom Fiber Englewood.

1978 — Green Manufacturing Origins

The green-first manufacturing model that defines this facility's DNA is established in Canada — converting automotive PVC trim scrap into reinstallable vehicle components instead of landfill. This recycled-material philosophy carries forward through every ownership transition to today.

2017 — Englewood Groundbreaking

A 105,000+ sq. ft. facility breaks ground at 300 Lau Pkwy, Englewood, Ohio. The $18M purpose-built Tier 1 manufacturing plant is designed for compression molding, vacuum forming, and fiber processing of automotive NVH components.

2018 — Production Begins

The Englewood facility opens and begins production in Q1 2018, manufacturing NVH products — acoustic insulators, wheel liners, body plugs, air deflectors, and underbody covers — for Ford, Chrysler, Toyota, and other major OEMs using recycled materials.

2021 — Woodbridge Englewood (NVH Div.)

The facility operates as Woodbridge Englewood, NVH Division. Capabilities expand with additional lightweight materials for next-generation NVH and EV platform applications.

2023 — Angstrom Fiber Englewood

Angstrom Automotive Group acquires the compressed fiber operations. The facility becomes Angstrom Fiber Englewood — a division of Angstrom Fiber Group under Angstrom NA — joining a global network of 15 companies and 33+ locations across three continents.
