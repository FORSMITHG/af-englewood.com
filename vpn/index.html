Full instructions

Step-by-step setup

Written for non-technical users. Follow each step in order.

01

Download the WARP client

Go to the official Cloudflare download page and download the Windows version of the Cloudflare WARP client.

Open download page ↗
02

Install the app

Open the downloaded installer and follow the prompts. After installation, look for the orange Cloudflare icon near the clock in the bottom-right corner of Windows.

03

Open Cloudflare WARP

Click the Cloudflare icon near the system clock. Open Settings or Preferences, go to Account, then choose Login to Cloudflare Zero Trust.

04

Enter the team name

When asked for the team name, enter only the following:

af-englewood

Do not enter the full website address — only the team name above.

05

Sign in with your Angstrom email

Enter your Angstrom email address, then click Send login code. You do not need to create a Cloudflare account.

06

Use the one-time login code

Check your email for a Cloudflare Access message. Copy the one-time code, return to the login page, and finish signing in.

07

Turn WARP on

After signing in, make sure WARP shows as connected. Once connected, approved internal resources can be reached using their normal internal address.

Important

What to remember

No Cloudflare account required

If you see a Cloudflare sign-up page, stop and open the WARP client instead.

Use your Angstrom email

Only approved Angstrom email addresses can receive a login code and connect.

Access is monitored

Remote access is protected, logged, and intended for authorized business use only.

Troubleshooting

Common issues

I do not see the Cloudflare icon.

Restart your computer and check the bottom-right corner near the Windows clock. You may need to click the up-arrow to show hidden icons.

It asks me to create a Cloudflare account.

You are on the wrong screen. Open the WARP client, choose Login to Cloudflare Zero Trust, and enter the team name af-englewood.

I did not receive a login code.

Check your Junk or Spam folder. The email should come from Cloudflare Access. If it still does not arrive, contact IT support.

My code does not work.

The one-time code may have expired. Request a new login code and try again.

I am connected but cannot reach anything.

Confirm WARP is turned on and connected to the af-englewood team. If it still does not work, contact IT support with the resource you are trying to reach.

Need help?

Contact IT Support

Include your name, device, whether WARP shows as connected, and the resource you are trying to reach.
